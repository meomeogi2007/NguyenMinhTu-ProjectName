Welcome to the Five Nights at Freddy's Wiki!
Welcome to the Five Nights at Freddy's Wiki - The inclusive encyclopedia about the widely popular horror franchise, for fans and gamers alike, where histories, strategical guides, secrets, and every pieces of lore are documented.

Five Nights at Freddy's is a media franchise based around an indie point-and-click horror video game series created by Scott Cawthon. The series is centered on the universe of various fictional restaurant chains (pastiches of real-life restaurants such as Chuck E. Cheese's and ShowBiz Pizza Place) hosted by the Fazbear Entertainment company.
